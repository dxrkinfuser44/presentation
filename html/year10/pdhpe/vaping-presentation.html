Health
Impacts
of Vaping

Electronic devices that heat liquid into aerosol
Contains nicotine, chemicals and flavourings
Not a safe alternative to smoking

Effects on the body

Short-Term
Effects

01
Sore throat, coughing and dizziness, common in new and regular users
02
Increased heart rate and blood pressure immediately after use
03
Nausea and headaches, particularly from nicotine exposure
02 / 05
Cumulative damage

Long-Term
Risks

01
Lung damage and reduced lung function from toxic aerosol particles
02
Nicotine addiction; the brain rewires to depend on the substance
03
Higher risk of heart disease from sustained cardiovascular stress
03 / 05
Youth & development

Why Young People
Are at Greater Risk

01
Developing brains are far more vulnerable to nicotine than adult brains
02
Addiction can take hold faster during adolescence
03
Early nicotine use is linked to anxiety, mood disorders and poor concentration

Take this with you

Key Message

01
Vaping damages your lungs, heart, and brain, no matter how it's marketed
02
No vaping product has been proven safe, especially for young people
03
The best choice is simply to not vape.
